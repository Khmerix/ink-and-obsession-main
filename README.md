# ink-and-obsession
An interactive horror novel
Summary of Ink and Obsession
Ink and Obsession is a haunting exploration of creativity, desire, and the dangerous allure of permanence. At its heart, the book follows a protagonist whose passion for ink—whether on paper or skin—becomes a consuming fixation. As artistry transforms into obsession, the story unravels the fine line between inspiration and compulsion, beauty and destruction.

Through vivid imagery and psychological depth, the narrative examines how art can both liberate and imprison, leaving indelible marks not only on the body but on the soul. Readers are drawn into a world where every stroke of ink carries weight, and every choice leaves a lasting imprint.

Themes:

The duality of creativity and obsession

Identity and self-expression through art

The permanence of choices and their consequences

Tone:  
Darkly lyrical, introspective, and emotionally charged—perfect for readers who enjoy psychological drama and character-driven storytelling.
